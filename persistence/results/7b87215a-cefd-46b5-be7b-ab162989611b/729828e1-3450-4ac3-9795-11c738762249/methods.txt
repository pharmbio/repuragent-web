Integrated ranking methodology (Data Agent)

Inputs:
- CP_AB_raw: morphology score (higher is better), infection rate % (lower is better). Mostly single concentration (10 µM).
- CPE_raw: inhibition of cytopathicity % (higher is better) measured across multiple concentrations (nM→µM); plus non-infected cell viability % as a cytotoxicity proxy.
- compound_annotations: compound_name ↔ chembl_id mapping.

Preprocessing:
- Removed non-data header rows embedded in the raw XLSX sheets.
- Normalized compound names to lower-case/trim; merged CP/AB and CPE to annotations by normalized name; kept all annotated compounds (N=5159).
- Converted CPE concentrations from nM to µM.

Per-compound summarization:
- CP: cp_best_score = max morphology score across rows (usually one row); ab_best_infection = min infection rate.
- CPE: cpe_best_inhib = max inhibition of cytopathicity; cpe_min_viability = minimum non-infected viability observed across concentrations; cpe_viability_at_best = viability at concentration where cpe_best_inhib occurs.

Normalization:
- Used percentile ranks (0–1) for robustness to different scales and outliers:
  * cp_norm = percentile rank of cp_best_score (higher better)
  * ab_norm = percentile rank of -ab_best_infection (lower infection better)
  * cpe_norm = percentile rank of cpe_best_inhib (higher better)

Composite score:
- composite_score = 0.25*cp_norm + 0.40*ab_norm + 0.35*cpe_norm - cytotoxicity_penalty
- cytotoxicity_penalty: up to 0.2 if cpe_min_viability <80% (linear scaling); 0 otherwise.

User priority flags:
- flag_cpe_gt15: cpe_best_inhib > 15%
- flag_ab_lt60: ab_best_infection < 60%
- flag_cp_top150: cp_best_score within top 150 compounds (threshold = 0.47039).
- priority_hits_count = number of these criteria met (0–3).

Final ranking:
- Sorted by priority_hits_count (desc) then composite_score (desc); includes all compounds (no exclusions).

Confounder flags (heuristic):
- flag_cytotox_strong: cpe_min_viability <70%
- flag_cytotox_moderate: 70–80%
- flag_ab_good_but_cytotox: AB hit with cpe_min_viability <80% (possible apparent antiviral via cell loss)
- flag_cp_only / flag_ab_only / flag_cpe_only: single-assay ‘wins’ without support from the other two assays
- flag_cpe_best_at_max_conc: CPE best effect observed at 10 µM (possible concentration artifact)
