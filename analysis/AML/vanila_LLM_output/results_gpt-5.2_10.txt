## Practical IC₅₀ assay setup (to make these ranges useful)
- **Dose-response design:** 10-point, **1:3 serial dilution**, top dose as listed.  
- **Exposure time:** **72 h** is common for MOLM-13 proliferation IC₅₀; optionally also 48 h.  
- **Readout:** CellTiter-Glo or resazurin; confirm with live cell counts/flow if compounds affect metabolism.  
- **Solvent controls:** Keep **DMSO constant** (e.g., 0.1% v/v).  
- **Replicates:** ≥3 technical, ≥3 biological repeats for IC₅₀ confidence.
