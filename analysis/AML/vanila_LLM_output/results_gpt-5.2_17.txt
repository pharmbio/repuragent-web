## Notes specific to MOLM‑13 (why these are prioritized)
- MOLM‑13 is **FLT3‑ITD** and is typically **highly sensitive** to **FLT3 inhibitors** (gilteritinib/quizartinib/midostaurin/crenolanib/ponatinib), often showing low‑nM growth inhibition.
- MOLM‑13 is frequently used in **venetoclax** studies; response can depend on culture conditions and metabolic state.
- **DNMT inhibitors** (azacitidine/decitabine) can show weaker short‑term IC₅₀s at 72 h; their clinically relevant effects often reflect longer exposure/schedule.
