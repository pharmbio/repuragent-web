## Notes to make these ranges “experimentally testable” in a real IC₅₀ assay

- **Use a broad initial range if unsure:** 0.001–30 µM (or 0.0003–10 µM for very potent kinase/HDAC inhibitors) catches most behaviors.
- **Vehicle (DMSO) control:** keep constant across wells (commonly ≤0.1% v/v).
- **Timepoint:** MOLM‑13 often shows clearer separation at **72 h** (sometimes 96 h for slower-acting epigenetic/autophagy agents).
- **Curve design:** 10 points, 1:3 dilutions is a good compromise (or half‑log).
- **Cytostatic vs cytotoxic:** CDK/mTOR/HDAC drugs may reduce ATP readouts without outright killing; consider annexin V/PI follow-up.
