## Important caveats that will affect whether you actually see inhibition in MOLM‑13
- **Serum binding** can shift apparent potency (notably venetoclax, navitoclax, sorafenib).  
- **Drug efflux** and transporter expression vary by line.  
- **Exposure time**: hypomethylating agents often look much stronger with **≥96 h** or repeated dosing.  
- **FLT3 inhibitors** generally have high likelihood in MOLM‑13, but resistance can emerge depending on culture conditions.
