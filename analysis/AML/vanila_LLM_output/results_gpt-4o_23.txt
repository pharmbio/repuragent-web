=== AML Drug Repurposing Suggestions ===

Prompt:
Suggest top 20 existing drugs that could be repurposed for acute myeloid leukemia (AML) treatment 
and provide experimentally testable concentrations for an IC50 assay. 
The drug should inhibit the proliferation of AML cell lines, particularly MOLM13.


==================================================

Results:

Repurposing existing drugs for the treatment of acute myeloid leukemia (AML) offers a promising strategy to accelerate drug development by bypassing early-phase testing. Below is a list of 20 existing drugs that have been explored or have potential for repurposing in AML treatment, especially against cell lines like MOLM13. The IC50 values provided are typical ranges or estimates that can be experimentally verified; they should ultimately be tailored based on specific experimental conditions such as the assay format and cell line used.

   - Suggested IC50 range: 1-10 μM

3. **Metformin**: An anti-diabetic drug being investigated for anti-cancer properties.

6. **Arsenic Trioxide**: Used in some leukemias, being tested for AML.
