### Notes to make these ranges “assay-ready”
- **Default screening series (recommended):** 10 points, 3-fold dilutions: **30 µM → 0.0015 µM (1.5 nM)**.  
  - For ultra-potent kinase inhibitors (midostaurin/ponatinib/trametinib/panobinostat), also consider adding a **0.3 nM** point.  
  - For **valproic acid**, use a separate series: **10 mM → 0.02 mM**.
- **Vehicle:** most are DMSO-soluble; keep **DMSO constant** across wells (often ≤0.1% v/v for MOLM-13).
- **Exposure time:** IC50s for AML viability are commonly measured at **72 h** (sometimes 96 h).
- **Readout:** CellTiter-Glo is common for suspension AML lines like MOLM-13.

- **MOLM-13 is FLT3-ITD driven:** FLT3/MAPK/mTOR/HDAC-directed agents tend to be more enriched for activity than random repurposing lists.
