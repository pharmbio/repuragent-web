## Notes to make the IC50 data interpretable
- **Match top concentration to solubility and protein binding.** Some compounds precipitate above ~10–30 µM in aqueous media.  
- **Use two independent viability measures** (e.g., ATP + cell counting or Annexin V) because some drugs change metabolism without killing.  
- **Include positive controls** (e.g., venetoclax, midostaurin, gilteritinib) and a **DMSO-only control**.  
- **MOLM‑13 is often FLT3‑ITD–driven**, so FLT3 inhibitors can appear exceptionally potent; confirm by comparing with a FLT3‑WT AML line.
