=== AML Drug Repurposing Suggestions ===

Prompt:
Suggest top 20 existing drugs that could be repurposed for acute myeloid leukemia (AML) treatment 
and provide experimentally testable concentrations for an IC50 assay. 
The drug should inhibit the proliferation of AML cell lines, particularly MOLM13.


==================================================

Results:

Below are 20 approved drugs with published antiproliferative activity in AML cell lines (many with specific data in FLT3-ITD models like MOLM-13). For each, I suggest an IC50 assay test range as a 10-point, 3-fold serial dilution starting from the “top” concentration given. The resulting bottom concentration is approximately top/19,683.

Notes:
- MOLM-13 is FLT3-ITD and MLL-rearranged; FLT3/multikinase and SYK inhibitors often show strong activity. 
- Use DMSO vehicle controls; check solubility and protein binding (e.g., venetoclax is highly protein-bound).
- Typical viability readouts at 72 h are common for AML cell lines; adjust as needed.

1) Sorafenib (multikinase/FLT3; approved for HCC, RCC)
- Strong activity in FLT3-ITD AML including MOLM-13.
- IC50 assay range: top 1 μM (≈50 pM–1 μM).

2) Ponatinib (BCR-ABL/FLT3; CML/Ph+ ALL)
- Very potent vs FLT3-ITD AML lines, including MOLM-13.
- IC50 assay range: top 300 nM (≈15 pM–300 nM).

3) Sunitinib (multikinase/FLT3; RCC, GIST)
- Inhibits FLT3-ITD AML proliferation.
- IC50 assay range: top 3 μM (≈150 pM–3 μM).

4) Cabozantinib (multikinase incl. FLT3; RCC, thyroid)
- Potent in FLT3-ITD AML cell lines including MOLM-13.
- IC50 assay range: top 1 μM (≈50 pM–1 μM).

5) Lenvatinib (VEGFR/RET/FGFR; thyroid, HCC, RCC)
- Reported FLT3 inhibition; active in FLT3-ITD AML models.
- IC50 assay range: top 5 μM (≈250 pM–5 μM).

6) Nintedanib (VEGFR/FGFR/PDGFR; IPF)
- Preclinical activity in FLT3-ITD AML lines including MOLM-13.
- IC50 assay range: top 5 μM (≈250 pM–5 μM).

7) Dasatinib (SRC/ABL; CML/Ph+ ALL)
- Inhibits SRC-family signaling; activity reported in MLL/FLT3 AML lines.
- IC50 assay range: top 1 μM (≈50 pM–1 μM).

8) Fostamatinib (SYK inhibitor; ITP; active metabolite R406)
- SYK dependency noted in MLL-rearranged AML; activity in MOLM-13.
- IC50 assay range: top 30 μM (≈1.5 nM–30 μM).

9) Ruxolitinib (JAK1/2; myelofibrosis, PV)
- Variable single-agent activity in AML; worth testing, esp. with cytokine-driven signaling.
- IC50 assay range: top 10 μM (≈0.5 nM–10 μM).

10) Trametinib (MEK1/2; melanoma)
- MAPK pathway inhibition shows AML line activity; test alone and in combinations.
- IC50 assay range: top 1 μM (≈50 pM–1 μM).

11) Selumetinib (MEK1/2; NF1-associated PN)
- MEK inhibition can suppress AML proliferation in subsets; test on MOLM-13.
- IC50 assay range: top 10 μM (≈0.5 nM–10 μM).

12) Venetoclax (BCL-2; CLL/SLL, AML)
- Originally approved for CLL; many AML lines respond variably; include due to clinical relevance.
- IC50 assay range: top 10 μM (≈0.5 nM–10 μM).

13) Bortezomib (proteasome; multiple myeloma, MCL)
- Potent AML cell line cytotoxicity including MOLM-13.
- IC50 assay range: top 100 nM (≈5 pM–100 nM).

14) Carfilzomib (proteasome; multiple myeloma)
- Strong proteasome-dependent killing in AML lines.
- IC50 assay range: top 100 nM (≈5 pM–100 nM).

15) Omacetaxine mepesuccinate (protein synthesis inhibitor; CML)
- Broad antileukemic activity; effective in AML cell lines.
- IC50 assay range: top 100 nM (≈5 pM–100 nM).

16) Vorinostat/SAHA (HDAC inhibitor; CTCL)
- HDAC inhibition reduces AML proliferation; activity in MOLM-13 reported.
- IC50 assay range: top 20 μM (≈1 nM–20 μM).

17) Panobinostat (HDAC inhibitor; multiple myeloma)
- Very potent pan-HDAC inhibitor; active in AML lines.
- IC50 assay range: top 1 μM (≈50 pM–1 μM).

18) Belinostat (HDAC inhibitor; PTCL)
- Cytotoxic to AML cell lines; test alongside other epigenetic agents.
- IC50 assay range: top 20 μM (≈1 nM–20 μM).

19) Tigecycline (mitochondrial translation inhibitor; antibiotic)
- Selectively impairs OxPhos in AML, including MOLM-13.
- IC50 assay range: top 50 μM (≈2.5 nM–50 μM).

20) Auranofin (thioredoxin reductase inhibitor; rheumatoid arthritis)
- Induces oxidative stress–mediated killing in AML cell lines.
- IC50 assay range: top 10 μM (≈0.5 nM–10 μM).

Optional additions/substitutions if you want a broader panel:
- Mefloquine (antimalarial; lysosomal cell death; test top 30 μM).
- Niclosamide (anthelmintic; mitochondrial uncoupler/Wnt inhibition; test top 10 μM).
- Clofazimine (antimycobacterial; mitochondrial/ion channel effects; test top 5 μM).
