## Notes to improve your chance of finding real MOLM‑13 hits
- **MOLM‑13 is FLT3‑ITD**, so **FLT3 inhibitors** are the most reliable positive class (quizartinib/gilteritinib/midostaurin/sorafenib).  
- **Venetoclax sensitivity** varies by culture conditions and anti-apoptotic context; consider testing **venetoclax + azacitidine** or **+ midostaurin** if single-agent effect is modest.
- Some drugs above have **solubility limits** (e.g., panobinostat, ponatinib). Don’t force top doses that visibly precipitate; cap where solutions stay clear in your media.
- Consider running **24 h vs 72 h** for drugs with rapid cytotoxicity (kinase inhibitors) versus **time-dependent epigenetic agents** (azacitidine/decitabine).
