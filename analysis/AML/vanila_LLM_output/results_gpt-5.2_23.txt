## Notes specific to MOLM‑13
- **MOLM‑13 is FLT3‑ITD positive**, so **FLT3-directed multi-kinase inhibitors** (e.g., midostaurin, sorafenib, sunitinib) and **BCL‑2 axis inhibitors** (venetoclax) are common high-priority repurposing directions.
- Many of the other agents show **context-dependent** activity (often stronger in combinations), so they’re reasonable in a repurposing screen but may not yield low single-agent IC₅₀s.
