## Notes to make your screen interpretable (without prescribing a protocol)
- **MOLM‑13** is typically highly responsive to **FLT3 inhibitors**; include at least one **FLT3‑ITD negative** AML line (e.g., HL‑60/THP‑1) as a specificity counterpoint.
- Some agents (e.g., **azacitidine/decitabine**) can look weak in short viability assays because their effects are **schedule- and replication-dependent**.
- For each drug, the practically usable top concentration is often constrained by **solubility** and **vehicle tolerance (e.g., DMSO)**; cap doses accordingly based on your SOP.
- Consider including **positive controls** (e.g., gilteritinib for MOLM‑13; staurosporine as a general cytotoxic control) and a **vehicle-only** condition.
