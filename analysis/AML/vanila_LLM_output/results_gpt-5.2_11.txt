## Practical notes (so the assay actually works)

### Solubility / handling flags
- **Niclosamide, mebendazole, albendazole, itraconazole/posaconazole**: low aqueous solubility; make fresh DMSO stocks (often 10–50 mM) and avoid precipitation (warm, vortex, filter if needed).
- **Valproic acid** is typically prepared in water or as sodium salt; mM testing is normal.

### MOLM13-specific tip
MOLM13 is **FLT3-ITD–driven** and often very sensitive to **FLT3 inhibitors**—but many FLT3 inhibitors are already “AML drugs” rather than repurposing candidates. If you want, I can add a separate list of **non-AML-approved kinase inhibitors** (originally for other cancers) that frequently show **low-nM to low-µM** MOLM13 activity.
