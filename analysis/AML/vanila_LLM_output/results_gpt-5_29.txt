=== AML Drug Repurposing Suggestions ===

Prompt:
Suggest top 20 existing drugs that could be repurposed for acute myeloid leukemia (AML) treatment 
and provide experimentally testable concentrations for an IC50 assay. 
The drug should inhibit the proliferation of AML cell lines, particularly MOLM13.


==================================================

Results:

Below are 20 existing, non-AML-approved drugs with published anti-AML activity (including activity in FLT3-ITD models like MOLM13). For each, I list the mechanism and a practical concentration range to span when generating an IC50 curve in MOLM13. Ranges are chosen to bracket reported potencies and accommodate variability; use 8–12 points across the span. Actual potency will depend on assay conditions, serum, and compound handling/solubility.

FLT3/MAPK/multikinase–pathway inhibitors (especially relevant to FLT3-ITD MOLM13)
- Sorafenib (multi-kinase; inhibits FLT3): Range 0.1 nM to 3 µM
- Ponatinib (ABL/FLT3/others): Range 0.1 nM to 3 µM
- Sunitinib (multi-kinase; FLT3): Range 0.5 nM to 10 µM
- Cabozantinib (MET/AXL/VEGFR; reported FLT3/AXL activity in AML): Range 0.5 nM to 10 µM
- Regorafenib (multi-kinase; reported FLT3/TIE2): Range 0.5 nM to 10 µM
- Dasatinib (SRC/ABL family; AML activity via SRC/KIT signaling): Range 0.5 nM to 10 µM
- Trametinib (MEK1/2 inhibitor): Range 0.1 nM to 3 µM
- Selumetinib (MEK1/2 inhibitor): Range 1 nM to 10 µM

PI3K/mTOR pathway
- Everolimus (mTORC1 inhibitor): Range 1 nM to 30 µM
- Copanlisib (pan-PI3K inhibitor): Range 1 nM to 30 µM

Proteasome inhibitors
- Bortezomib (26S proteasome): Range 0.5 nM to 100 nM
- Carfilzomib (irreversible proteasome inhibitor): Range 0.5 nM to 300 nM
- Ixazomib (oral proteasome inhibitor): Range 1 nM to 1 µM

Epigenetic modulators
- Panobinostat (pan-HDAC inhibitor): Range 0.5 nM to 1 µM
- Vorinostat/SAHA (HDAC inhibitor): Range 0.05 µM to 20 µM
- Romidepsin (class I HDAC inhibitor): Range 0.1 nM to 0.5 µM

Nuclear export inhibitor
- Selinexor (XPO1/CRM1 inhibitor): Range 1 nM to 5 µM

Mitochondrial/OxPhos and metabolic inhibitors
- Tigecycline (mitochondrial translation inhibitor; antibiotic): Range 0.1 µM to 30 µM
- Atovaquone (OxPhos complex III inhibitor; antimalarial): Range 0.1 µM to 30 µM
- Niclosamide (oxidative phosphorylation/Wnt/STAT3; anthelmintic): Range 0.01 µM to 10 µM

Redox/other
- Auranofin (thioredoxin reductase inhibitor; anti-rheumatic): Range 0.01 µM to 10 µM

Notes
- MOLM13 is FLT3-ITD; FLT3-directed and downstream pathway inhibitors (first two sections) are often especially potent.
- For poorly soluble compounds (e.g., niclosamide, atovaquone), ensure the top concentration is within solubility limits in your assay medium; if precipitation is seen, lower the top concentration.
- If you need alternatives to reach 20 with different mechanisms, additional repurposing candidates with AML activity include omacetaxine mepesuccinate (homoharringtonine; approved for CML; suggest 0.5 nM–10 µM) and ruxolitinib (JAK1/2; suggest 0.01 µM–30 µM).
