## Practical notes to make these “experimentally testable” in MOLM-13
- **Vehicle/solvent:** Many of these are DMSO stocks (10–20 mM). Keep final DMSO consistent across wells.
- **Plate density:** MOLM-13 often works well at ~5,000–20,000 cells/well (96-well), depending on readout and duration.
- **Readout choice:** CellTiter-Glo is robust for suspension AML lines; verify linearity with a cell-number standard curve.
- **Controls:** Include a cytotoxic positive control (e.g., staurosporine at 0.1–1 µM) and a MOLM-13–relevant control (FLT3 inhibitor).
- **Edge cases:**  
  - **Valproate** requires **mM** dosing; include matched osmolarity controls if needed.  
  - **Nucleoside analogs** (azacitidine/decitabine) can show time- and media-dependent effects; 72 h (or longer) and fresh prep improves interpretability.
